## Key findings

1. **Dark tokens (6M-0) match `[data-theme="dark"]` 1:1** on every bg / accent / semantic swatch.
2. **Light surface tokens (9J-0) drift from `:root`** on `--color-bg-primary`, `--color-bg-elevated`, `--color-bg-panel`, `--color-bg-hover`. The in-code comment says "cool neutral canvas … D23 layer reversal"; Paper shows the opposite (warm cream ground, white elevated). Also contradicts D23 as written. Rank: HIGH — biggest single visual-fidelity gap.
3. **Spacing + radius + font-size scales match Paper exactly.** `space-5` / `space-10` unused in Paper but coverage-gap, not drift.
4. **Typography weight gap**: Paper light renders display at Host Grotesk 700/28; dark at 600/28. No `--font-weight-display` token exists to let a primitive opt into the theme-dependent weight.
5. **Primitives cover ~70% of Paper's use-cases**. Missing: workspace-grade Tab/TabBar (HIGH), chat Composer (HIGH), rail-style 36×36 accent-soft IconButton variant (MED), split-pill folder+chevron (MED), tag/chip input (LOW, post-MVP), Select (LOW, post-MVP).
6. **ContextBadge semantic drift**: Paper uses neutral chip + separate streaming dot; in-code couples state into the chip fill. Recommend re-splitting.
7. **Badge radius drift**: Paper pills are `radius-pill`; in-code default is `radius-md`. Caller opt-in to pill shape recommended.
8. **SearchInput height**: inherits TextInput's 44px; Paper search rows are 32px. Needs explicit compact variant.
